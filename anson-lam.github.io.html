Bootcamp 2020: E-Blind

This is my Processing project of the 2-week Coding Bootcamp at City, E-Blind. It demonstrates my understanding of some programming skills, including users' interaction, loops, and conditionals. On the background of a beautiful view, I created a digital blind, which is made of a number of regular rectangles. In fact, the E-Blind can be easily used. When you intend to block the light outside the "window", or simply want more privacy, click anywhere to reduce the tranparency of the built-in digital blind gradually. Missing the beautiful view? Just press the space bar to increase the level of transparency. Stay tuned for more features, from selecting your own colour of the E-Blind anytime to addition visual effects while changing the transparency.
